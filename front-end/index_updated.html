The psalms of lament form the largest category in the Psalter — David did not shy from grief. Psalm 22 opens with the cry of desolation that Jesus himself echoed from the cross. Psalm 88 is the darkest, ending without resolution. Psalm 34:18 promises the Lord is near to the brokenhearted. Begin there.
